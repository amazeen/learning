Apply Blood of Jesus

Derek Prince

Satan is afraid of committed believers, believer's number one priority is to do the will of God not whether they stay alive or not. Revelations 12:11
Blood of Jesus - needs to be applied to us and we must be obedient, we must have fellowship with God (we must be in the light, the Blood does not clean in the dark 1 John 1:7)

Blood of Jesus provides:

  • Redemption
  • Cleansing - 1 John 1:7
  • Justification - acquitted, not guilty, you been reckoned righteous with righteousness of Jesus Christ not your own, made righteous. Romans 5:9 I’m justified means as if I had never sinned. Robe of His righteousness (Isaiah 61:10)
  • Sanctification - separate from sin, set apart to God, made holy with God’s own Holiness. Hebrews 13:12, Hebrews 12:10
  • Life - Leviticus 17:11, God’s own life is in the Blood of Jesus, John 6:53-57 verily verily means very important, we eat His flesh and drink His blood when we take communion, 1 Corinthians 10:16, 1 Corinthians 11:23-26
  • Intercession - Hebrews 12:22-24, sprinkled blood of Jesus is always speaking on our behalf
  • Access - we have access through the Blood of Jesus, Hebrews 10:19-23

I/We can declare:

1. Through the blood of Jesus, I have been redeemed out of the hand of the devil.
2. While I walk in the light, the Blood of Jesus cleanses me now and continually from all sins.
3. Through the blood of Jesus, I am justified, acquitted, not guilty, reckoned righteous, made righteous, just as if I never sinned. Thank you Jesus. Thank you God.
4. hrough the blood of Jesus, I am sanctified, separated from sin, set apart to God, made holy with God’s Holiness.
5. Lord Jesus, when I receive your Blood, in it, I receive Your life, the life of God, divine, eternal, endless life. Thank you Jesus. Thank you Lord.
6. Thank you Lord that even when I cannot pray, the blood of Jesus is pleading for me in heaven. Amen.
7. I consistently bring God encounters to other people. Mark 16:17,18; Acts 3:6 Thank you Lord that through the sprinkled blood of Jesus, I have access into the Presence of Almighty God, into the Holiest Place in the universe.


我們來宣告：

1. 藉著耶穌的寶血，我已從撒旦的手中被救贖出來。
2. 當我行在光中時，耶穌的寶血現在並持續地洗淨我所有的罪。
3. 藉著耶穌的寶血，我被稱為義、被稱為無罪、算為義、成為義人，就好像我從未犯過罪一樣。 感謝耶穌。
4. 藉著耶穌的寶血，我得以成聖，與罪分離，歸給神，因神的聖潔而成為聖潔。
5. 主耶穌，當我接受祢的寶血時，我就在其中接受了祢的生命，神的生命，神聖、永恆、無盡的生命。感謝耶穌。
6. 主啊，感謝祢，即使我無法禱告，耶穌的寶血仍在天上為我祈求。阿們。
7. 主啊，感謝祢，藉著耶穌所灑的寶血，我得以進入全能神的面前，進入宇宙中的至聖所。
