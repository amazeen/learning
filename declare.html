My Powerful Declarations

Steve and Wendy Backlund

1. My prayers are powerful and effective. 2Corinthians 5:21; James 5:16b
2. God richly supplies all my needs. Philippians 4:19
3. I am dead to sin and have a victorious DNA in me. Romans 6:11; Romans 5:17
4. I walk in ever increasing health. Isaiah 53:3-5; Psalm 103:1-5
5. I live under a supernatural protection. Psalm 91; Hebrews 8:6
6. I prosper in all my relationships. Luke 2:52
7. I consistently bring God encounters to other people. Mark 16:17,18; Acts 3:6
8. In Jesus, I am 100% loved and worthy to experience all of God’s blessings. Col. 1:12-15
9. Each of my family members is wonderfully blessed and radically loves Jesus. Acts 16:30, 31
10. I uproariously laugh when I hear a lie from the devil. Psalm 2:2-4
11. I set the course of my life with my words. James 3:2-5; Proverbs 18:21
12. God is on my side; therefore, I declare that I cannot be defeated. Romans 8:37; Psalm 91; Philippians 4:13
13. I am the head, not the tail. I have insight. I have wisdom. I have ideas and divine strategies. I have blueprints downloaded from heaven. I have authority. Deut. 8:18; 28:13; James 1:5-8;Luke 10:19
14. My family and those connected to us are protected from disasters, disease, divorce, adultery, poverty, false accusation, foolish decisions and all accidents. Psalm 91
15. As Abraham did, I speak God’s promises over my life so that my faith is strengthened to possess all of God’s promises. Romans 4:17-23
16. I have a sound mind. I think the right thoughts, say the right words, have the right attitudes, and make the right decisions in every situation I face. 2 Timothy 1:7
17. I expect to have powerful, divine appointments today to heal the sick, raise the dead, prophesy life, lead people to Christ, bring deliverance, release signs and wonders, and bless every place I go. The book of Acts
18. I expect that today will be the best day of my life spiritually, emotionally, relationally, financially, and physically in Jesus’ name. Romans 15:13
19. Thank you angels for carrying out the Word of God on my behalf. Psalm 103:2
20. All attacks that were headed my way are diverted right now through angelic protection in Jesus’ name. Psalm 91
21. Just as Jesus spoke to the wind, I say, “Peace, be still to my mind, emotions, body and family.” Mark 4:39
22. I speak to every mountain of discouragement, stress, depression and lack, and say, “Be cast it into the sea in Jesus’ name.” Mark 11:22-24
23. I say to this day, “You are blessed!” And I declare that I serve a mighty God, who today will do exceedingly and abundantly beyond all that I can ask or think. I say you are a good God, and I eagerly anticipate your goodness today. Ephesians 3:20
