  SI   Heart

When we truly see and speak with authentic discernment, people (the one you're talking to or praying for) will experience being connected to God's love, the sense of separateness to God's love will disappear for them. Bestowing dignity and honor to them. The words you speak, the value you have of them will express hope. It will impart love. It will embrace people into an experience with God. The gift of discernment will always produce fruit of His nature in a situation.

When I speak in true discernment, the person should encounter love, experience Him. If what I am about to say undermines or damages their reputation (the one I'm speaking about), then it's not discernment.
